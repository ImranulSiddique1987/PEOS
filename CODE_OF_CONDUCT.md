# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in the PEOS community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness.
- Respecting differing viewpoints.
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback.
- Taking responsibility for mistakes.
- Prioritizing the community.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or discrimination.
- Personal attacks.
- Trolling.
- Publishing private information.
- Any conduct inappropriate in a professional environment.

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct.
