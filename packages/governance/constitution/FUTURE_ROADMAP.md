# PEOS Future Roadmap

**Document Type:** Strategic Roadmap  
**Status:** Active  
**Applies To:** Entire PEOS Platform  
**Authority:** PEOS Governance Workspace

---

# Purpose

The PEOS Future Roadmap defines the long-term strategic direction of the Product Engineering Operating System (PEOS).

Unlike the Master Implementation Roadmap (PMIR), which governs the execution of approved milestones, this document captures the long-term engineering vision and strategic initiatives that guide the future evolution of the platform.

This roadmap represents **where PEOS is intended to evolve**, while the PMIR defines **how approved work is executed**.

---

# Strategic Vision

PEOS is being engineered as an AI-native Product Engineering Operating System capable of supporting the complete lifecycle of modern software products—from discovery and architecture through implementation, runtime execution, governance, operations, and continuous evolution.

Long-term engineering decisions should align with this vision.

---

# Strategic Principles

Future initiatives shall:

- Support the long-term platform vision.
- Preserve architectural integrity.
- Maintain repository stability.
- Maximize reuse through shared platform capabilities.
- Enable AI-native engineering workflows.
- Deliver measurable enterprise value.
- Avoid speculative implementation.
